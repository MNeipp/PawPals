Hey! We're Mason and Monty...

...the team behind PawPals. We're soon to be grads of Coding Dojo and we created this site because we knew that the new futures we're building for ourselves needed to include our 4-legged friends.


To create our doggie adoption site, we integrated with PetFinderAPI to bring you dogs who are looking for homes across the USA. Styling brought to you by Bootstrap4 and some really great stock images— courtesty of Pexels.com!


We hope you enjoyed your visit, and that you'll spread the word about PawPals to your friends and community. We'd love to help find forever-homes for each and every one of these lovable pups!

Just like PawPals, we are a constant work in progress :)
