Hey! We're Mason and Monty...


...the duo behind PawPals. We created this site because 2020 has been a tough year, and we think that having some extra love from a furry friend might make things a little better for everyone.

To create our doggie adoption site, we integrated with PetFinderAPI to bring you dogs who are looking for homes across the USA. Styling brought to you by Bootstrap4 and some really great stock images— courtesty of great photographers on Pexels and Unsplash!

We hope you enjoyed your visit, and that you'll spread the word about PawPals to your friends and community. We'd love to help find forever-homes for each and every one of these lovable pups!
